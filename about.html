    Driver Lab School

    We Teach You With A Smile

    Driver Lab School was founded in 2015 and started operating in Durban CBD in January 2016 after a thorough research about how to better serve customers. We are revolutionizing the industry by forcing our competitors to start paying more attention to quality driver training as opposed to obsessing with helping customers get their driver’s licenses “quickly”. The future belongs to networked businesses and Driver Lab School is looking for like-minded entrepreneurs to join and serve Africa.

    Our Vision

    Our vision is to enable, develop and equip drivers with good defensive driving skills in South Africa and beyond.

    Our Mission

    Our Mission is to offer excellent driver training, thereby preparing learner drivers both practically and psychologically to become safe and considerate drivers on South Africa’s roads.
